Project Portfolio

• Photo Composer •

Photo Composer is a smart-camera Android application that teaches the user how to take better photos by giving a comprehensive evaluation of the photos they capture.

• DC Concerts •

DC Concerts is an informative Android application for spontaneous concert-goers that congregates upcoming concerts in Washington DC and displays the most popular Spotify songs each artist has produced.

• Day Trip Planner •

Day Trip Planner is a useful Android application for finding certain food and attraction destinations around a specified area, so the user can plan their day out with ease.

• Simple Weather •

Simple Weather is an HCI-focused Android weather application that utilizes custom graphics and animations for an enjoyable and painless user experience.

• Dog Facts •

Dog Facts is an augmented reality iOS app for prospective dog owners to see how different dogs would look in their living space and interact with them through voice and tap commands.

• Corn Maze •

Corn Maze is an Oculus Quest virtual reality app simulates the experience of navigating through a corn maze and then picking up a mallet and striking a gong once the exit is found.
