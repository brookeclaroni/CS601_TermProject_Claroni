About

My name is Brooke Claroni, and I am from Reading, Massachussetts. I attended undergrad in Washington DC at George Washington University. I graduated from there in 2021 with a B.S. in Computer Science. Since then, I've moved back to Reading and have been attending Boston University for graduate school online. My anticipated graduation date is May 2023 for my M.S. in Software Development.

I took my first computer science course in 2015. It was the spring semester of my sophomore year in high school, and the course was an introductory class taught in Python. It quickly became my favorite class, and it was then that I had pretty much decided on what my career would be. While some parts of undergrad made me question this path, I knew it was right for me once I started taking my computer science elective courses. I'm excited to be in this graduate-level elective course now.
